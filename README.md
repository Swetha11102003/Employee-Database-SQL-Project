# Business Questions
1. Who is the highest-paid employee?
2. Which department has the highest average salary?
3. Which employees perform above average?
4. Which employees are eligible for promotion?
5. Which department has the best average performance?
6. Which employees joined after 2022?
7. Which department has the most employees?
8. Which employees receive the highest bonus?
9. Which employees earn above their department average?
10. Which departments need performance improvement?

# Business Insights
1. The highest-paid employee represents the top salary earner in the organization.
2. The department with the highest average salary indicates where the company invests     the most in employee compensation.
3. Employees performing above average are valuable contributors.
4. High-performing employees are potential promotion candidates.
5. The best-performing department demonstrates strong productivity.
6. Recent hires reflect company growth.
7. The largest department has the highest workforce.
8. Top bonus recipients are recognized for exceptional performance.
9. Employees earning above their department average are among the top earners in their    teams.
10. Departments with lower performance may require improvement initiatives.
